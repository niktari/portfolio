Paradigm is a hypothetical biannual journal that highlights the works, essays, and insights surrounding design, radical indigenism, and climate justice. About

Other Forms is a type tool that explores the generative potential of mold, rot, and decay through typographic form. The different growth settings are inspired by slime mold, reindeer lichen, and coral fungus, which form and influence the shape of the letters. The algorithm used to generate the growth is loosely based on Lindenmayer systems, a rewriting system that describes how living things can develop complex forms through the repeated application of simple rules. The base typeface used in the generator is Formula Condensed by Pangram Pangram.
