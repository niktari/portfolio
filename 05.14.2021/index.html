Nikki (Niktari) Makagiansar is a designer and coder based in Brooklyn, New York. She consistently finds herself in the space between design, art, technology, and community organizing.

In addition to working on freelance projects, Nikki is also a co-founder of SPICY, an arts and publishing organization led by women of color and queer and trans people of color. Prior to returning to school at Parsons School of Design to pursue a design practice, she was part of the brand marketing team at Comedy Central.

Currently, Nikki is exploring the possibilities of code as a creative medium and is experimenting with Processing and openFrameworks. Check out her sketchbook below.
