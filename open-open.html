OPEN OPEN

Identity, Design, Development

2021

OPEN OPEN is a tennis tournament that is open to anyone and everyone: amateurs, pros, people who have never picked up a racquet. A flexible identity system was created for the inaugural event.

Unlike major existing tennis opens, this tournament isn’t entirely driven by competition and accolades—there are no elimination rounds, barriers, or hierarchy. Instead, OPEN OPEN is driven by community connections and learning from one another through play, fun, and inclusivity.

To highlight the playful and accessible nature of the event, literal openings were created within the wordmark, with Karel Martens-inspired tennis balls pushing through the gaps. These elements can then be configured in multiple ways to create dynamic, visual marks applied across different spatial contexts, such as web, outdoor signage, tickets, and other various collateral.

About

Nikki (Niktari) Makagiansar is a designer who likes to code based in Brooklyn, New York. She enjoys combining motion, print, code, and typography to create bespoke visual systems and generative identities.

Nikki is a recent graduate of Parsons School of Design, where she was also a research assistant for the Design & Technology director, Justin Bakse.

She is also a co-founder of SPICY, a grassroots arts and publishing organization for and by queer and trans people of color. Prior to pursuing a design practice, she was part of the brand marketing team at Comedy Central.
