Interactive moments for a husband-and-wife design studio that reflect their type-driven approach.

R&M is a studio run by art directors and type designers Ryan Bugden and Michelle Ando. They approached us to develop interactive moments for their new studio site, including a homepage hero and supporting microinteractions.

We wanted to reflect the studio’s collaborative, dual approach through typographically-driven interactions that are both thoughtful and delightful.

We created a wide-ranging sketch library that included five hero concepts, each with 2-3 variants, and 2 microinteractions, also with variants.

This totaled to 18 sketches for consideration.
