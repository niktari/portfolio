Other Forms

Design, Development

2021

Other Forms is a type tool that explores the generative potential of mold, rot, and decay through typographic form. The different growth settings are inspired by slime mold, reindeer lichen, and coral fungus, which form and influence the shape of the letters.

The algorithm used to generate the growth is loosely based on Lindenmayer systems, a rewriting system that describes how living things can develop complex forms through the repeated application of simple rules.

The base typeface used in the generator is Formula Condensed by Pangram Pangram.

About

Nikki (Niktari) Makagiansar is a designer who likes to code based in Brooklyn, New York. She enjoys combining motion, print, code, and typography to create bespoke visual systems and generative identities.

Nikki is a recent graduate of Parsons School of Design, where she was also a research assistant for the Design & Technology director, Justin Bakse.

She is also a co-founder of SPICY, a grassroots arts and publishing organization for and by queer and trans people of color. Prior to pursuing a design practice, she was part of the brand marketing team at Comedy Central.
